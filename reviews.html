The Wonder Boys
reviews & interviews

 

The first two albums by The Wonder Boys was first
reviewed in Modern Rock Magazine #8 (March 2000) and
#9 (Jan 2002) by Tim Ellison.
You can order back issues of this wonderful magazine
through this web page, and The
Wonder Boys strongly recommend that you get ‘em all -
NOW!!

"And It’s A Wonderful, New Start" was reviewed in the
internet fanzine Ear Candy, May 2002 by Scott Homewood.

“II” was reviewed in a later issue of Ear Candy, Oct
2002 by Sean Koepenick. As you all know, 0,000 001 million
Wonder Boys fans can’t be wrong, but this guy
certainly can - and is. Can you count in how many
ways? Turn the computer upside-down for the right
answer!

...And let’s not forget that in the Village Voice’s
"Pazz & Jop" poll for best albums of the year 2000, The Wonder Boys "II"
shared a handsome 673:rd place!

Million Wonder Boys Fans Can’t Be Wrong” cd in issue
#9, winter 2002/2003. Written by Anders Dahlbom.
